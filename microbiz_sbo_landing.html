What is SBO?

Subscription-Based Outsourcing

SBO — Subscription-Based Outsourcing — is a model where businesses subscribe to the specialized services they need, instead of hiring full-time staff. Clients pay a recurring subscription for access to certified professionals, and you deliver on your own terms.

As a Microbiz SBO member, you are a certified specialist placed in an active business pool. Organizations come to Microbiz when they need technical support, project management, creative services, or operational expertise — without the cost and commitment of a full-time hire.

You build recurring income. They get exactly the talent they need. Everyone wins.

For Members

Recurring subscription income. Work with multiple clients. Set your own pace and scope.

For Clients

On-demand certified expertise. No payroll overhead. Scale up or down as needed.
